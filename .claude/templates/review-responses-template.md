## Response Guidelines

**Always:**
- Personalize with user's name and specific details from their review
- Acknowledge the feedback genuinely
- Provide a concrete next step or resolution
- Include support email for complex issues
- Keep responses under 350 characters (visible without "read more")

**Never:**
- Be defensive or argumentative
- Copy-paste identical responses to multiple reviews
- Promise features with specific timelines you can't guarantee
- Ignore negative reviews (they impact conversion rate)
- Ask users to change their rating directly

**Escalation Protocol:**
1. **1-2 star with bug:** Respond within 12 hours, create internal ticket
2. **1 star with legal threat:** Escalate to legal/management immediately
3. **Repeated same issue:** Escalate to development as priority fix
4. **Fake/spam review:** Report to Apple/Google for removal

**Review Response Cadence:**
- Check for new reviews: Daily
- Respond to negative reviews: Within 12 hours
- Respond to positive reviews: Within 24 hours
- Respond to neutral reviews: Within 24 hours
- Review response effectiveness: Monthly (track rating changes)
