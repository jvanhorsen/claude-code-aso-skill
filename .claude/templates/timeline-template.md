## Contingency Buffer

- **Apple rejection:** +3-5 days for fix and resubmission
- **Google rejection:** +5-10 days for fix and resubmission
- **Design delays:** Metadata can launch without final screenshots (update later)
- **Scope reduction:** If behind schedule, prioritize: metadata > screenshots > video > A/B tests
